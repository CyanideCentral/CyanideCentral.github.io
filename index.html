About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am Yiran Li, a fourth-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research focuses on graph mining algorithms, and I am particularly interested in developing efficient algorithms for analyzing and extracting meaningful patterns from large-scale graphs. I am also passionate about big data processing, graph learning, and applying AI techniques to advance scientific research.

  • Big Data Processing
